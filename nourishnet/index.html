Food is a human right.

Mission

Nourish Net connects surplus food from grocery stores to local food agencies such as food pantries and soup kitchens to empower communities around the United States.


What We Do

Outreach

We create partnerships between grocery stores and food agencies in communities without food rescue operations.

Onboard

We train employees at grocery stores to donate food in set up bins.

Coordination

We work with food agencies volunteers to pick up food from grocery stores according to their needs and schedule.
